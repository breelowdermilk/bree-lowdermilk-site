# Audience & Top Tasks
## Audiences
- **Fans & listeners** — stream signature tracks, see upcoming shows.
- **Vocalists/teachers/MDs** — find audition/cabaret pieces by voice type/range/key; buy sheet music.
- **Press & industry** — grab bio, photos, quotes, contact details.

## Top Tasks (Jobs to be Done)
1. Find a **song** that fits voice type/key and preview a recording.
2. Get **sheet music** link and key options quickly.
3. See **upcoming events** and add to calendar.
4. Access **press kit** with downloadable assets.
5. Read **project pages** (shows, albums) and navigate between related songs.
