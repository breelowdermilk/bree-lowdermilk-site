# Stack Decision — Astro + AstroWind + Pagefind
- **Rationale:** Markdown‑first authoring, $0 hosting, minimal debugging, fast and accessible defaults.
- **Alternatives considered:** Webflow/Squarespace (polish + speed to ship; monthly fees), Next.js+Sanity (power; overkill now).
- **Exit strategy:** Content lives in MDX; portable to any SSG or headless CMS later.
