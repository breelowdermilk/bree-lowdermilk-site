Mad to live, mad to talk, mad to be saved... Samantha Brown balances on the edge of her future, car keys in hand. As she sits in the driver's seat, she faces a choice: will she follow in her mother's footsteps, or take the dare of her impetuous best friend and chart a new path? This contemporary and compelling score from one of NYC's most exciting new songwriting teams immerses audiences in the complex inner life of a young woman on the brink of change. When every choice feels like life and death, how do you turn the key? The Mad Ones (fka The Unauthorized Autobiography of Samantha Brown) opened off-Broadway November 17, 2017. Licensing and album forthcoming. For professional inquiries, contact Seth Glewen at sglewen[at]gershny.com.
